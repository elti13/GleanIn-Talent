How It Works

Three steps.

Submit your profile once. We match you to projects that fit your skills.

STEP 01
Submit your profile

Tell us your skills, your availability, and what kind of work you want to do. Takes five minutes.

STEP 02
Get matched to a project

We connect you with a founder whose project fits your background. You review their listing, LinkedIn, track record. You decide.

STEP 03
Do the work. Get your stake.

Complete your milestones. Your deferred payment converts to an equity stake at their next funding round.

What You Get

More than a project.

Every engagement gives you three things most work never does.

Equity

Your work becomes an equity investment in a company you believe in. The value of your contribution converts into ownership when the startup raises its next round

Experience

Every project is with a real founder building something real. You walk away with a portfolio piece, a case study, and proof that you can deliver.

Network

The founder you work with today is your reference and your introduction to the next opportunity. Early-stage founders know other founders.
